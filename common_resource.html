Common Resources Guide

Introduction

Comprehensive list of common resources, how to acquire them, and how much of each resource you can acquire in a reasonable time frame of playing Azur Lane!


Notes

  • This guide assumes you have Merchant, Oil Canteen, Lecture Hall, and all Daily Raids to their maximum level.

  • Quantities are only listed if the resource is in high-demand, guaranteed, and/or as I deem fit.

    • Quantities assume a player has not spent money and/or gems on these resources.

    • Only guaranteed drops are considered. You can get more of these resources than listed depending on your RNG in commissions, shop rotations, map drops, etc.

    • If a quantity says "Not a concern," it means that it is almost never a limiting factor for mid/late-game players. Sorry beginners, you might still have scarcity issues with some of them.

    • Weeks are assumed to be 7 Days, Months are assumed to be 30 Days, and Months are assumed to be 4 Weeks long. This means quantities listed will be slightly underestimated due to how the calendar works.

  • Relevant Wiki Pages: Commissions, Cruise Missions, Daily Raid, Living Area, Missions, Shops.

Optimal Modules by Hull Type

For Ships with a Unique Augment Module, use their Unique Augment Module on them!

On U-557 and Minneapolis, their Unique Augments are sidegrades to Universal Augments, so there is no need to craft them.


DD / DDG

Augment Module Evaluation

Dual Swords

Best general-use DD augment; used on all DDs that aren't heavily reliant on Torpedo Damage.

Hammer

Best on DDs who are heavily reliant on Torpedo damage.

CL

Augment Module Evaluation

Crossbow

Offensive-oriented CL augment. Not much difference between Crossbow and Sword in practice.

Sword

Defensive-oriented CL augment. Not much difference between Crossbow and Sword in practice.

CB / CA

Augment Module Evaluation

Greatsword

Best general-use CA augment.

Lance

Better than Greatsword if you need the Hit stat, otherwise it's enhance fodder.

BB / BC / BBV

Augment Module Evaluation

Officer's Sword

Best general-use BB augment.

Bowgun

Outperforms Officer's Sword in the following cases (do your own research for breakpoints): against Heavy Armor bosses, against certain low-Evasion bosses, and/or when equipped on high-Hit ships.

CV / CVL

Augment Module Evaluation

Scepter

Always equip at least one per fleet. Not much difference between Scepter and Hunting Bow in practice. Equip to all CV / CVL in Chapter 14.

Bowgun

Equip to all CV / CVL that aren't using Scepter. Not much difference between Scepter and Hunting Bow in practice.

SS / SSV

Augment Module Evaluation

Dagger

Best general-use SS augment.

Kunai

Never craft this; always use as enhance fodder.
