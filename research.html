Shipyard

Shipyard UI

This is where you unlock and develop Shipyard ships.


Coin-up

All PR1, PR2/DR2, and PR3 ships can have their prints be bought with coins! See this wiki page for more information.


Recommended Shipyard Priority

  • See the Fleetbuilding Guide to see how ships fit into your fleet.

  • PR1, PR2/DR2, and PR3 prints can be bought with coins, so developing some of them first is highly recommended.

  • If you can't develop a ship due to Tech Point limitations, move on to the next ship and come back once you can develop them.

  • Bolded ships are the recommended Catch-up selections for that season.

    • For Catch-up Min Maxing, see INSERT LINK HERE for more information. Bolded ships are the recommended Catch-up Selections for the season. If a ship is not listed, but has a Catch-up (ex. Drake), do it last.


Recommended Development Order:

  • Friedrich der Große

  • Any PR1/PR2/PR3 ship

  • Anchorage

  • Any PR1/PR2/PR3 ship

  • Azuma

  • Rest of the ships

    • Prioritize ships you have blueprints for.

    • Always have a ship you can use coins on at all times.

    • Make sure to develop 1 ship each from PR1 - PR4 due to Beginner Rewards.


Shipyard Priority by Season:

  • PR1: Monarch > Saint Louis = Roon > rest

  • PR2: Kitakaze = Gascogne > rest

  • PR3: Odin >= Cheshire > Mainz > rest

  • PR4: Anchorage > August von Parseval > rest

  • PR5: Harbin = Chkalov > rest

    • PR5 catchup is not released yet.

  • PR6: Shimanto = Felix Schultz > rest

    • PR6 catchup is not released yet.

  • DRs: Friedrich der Große > Azuma > Plymouth > Ägir > rest

    • Since FdG and Azuma have coin-up, they are highest priority to acquire.

    • Get all other DRs at your own pace. Since they take forever to max, there is no rush.


Research Academy

See also: Research Academy


Research Academy UI

Research Focus UI

Purpose of Research

To get PR / DR Blueprints to use in Shipyard, as well as Equipment Designs to craft into gear.


Research Focus

When a season is focused, at least 3 out of the 5 available Research Projects will be from that season.


Research Focus Optimization:

Select PR4 or PR5 focus unless you know what you are doing. They have the best ships and gear.


Why not PR1, PR2, PR3?

  • All the PR ships in PR1, PR2, and PR3 can have their ship blueprints bought with coins directly, instead of having to research for them.

  • All the DR ships in DR2 can have their ship blueprints bought with coins directly, instead of having to research for them.

  • All the gear in PR1, PR2, PR3 are found in the Prototype Shop and/or the Gear Lab.


Why not PR6?

  • The gear in PR6 is not as important as the gear in PR4 / PR5.

  • The ships are comparable to other ships in PR4 / PR5.


Catch-up

When a ship is selected for catch-up, every research you complete will always give a minimum of 1 additional print that is for the catch-up ship selected.


What you get from Catch-up:

  • 300 total (PR) prints for a combination of any of the 6 PR1 ships.

  • 300 total (PR) prints for a combination of any of the 4 PR2 ships.

  • 300 total (PR) prints for a combination of any of the 4 PR3 ships.

  • 300 total (PR) prints for a combination of any of the 3 PR4 ships.

    • It's recommended that all 300 prints are for the same ship, until the ship reaches Development Level 30.

  • 150 (DR) prints for Friedrich der Große

  • 150 (DR) prints for Azuma

  • 150 (DR) prints for Drake

  • 150 (DR) prints for Ägir

  • 150 (DR) prints for Hakuryuu

You can only do one catch-up at a time, but there is no time limit. It is recommended to do the DR catch-ups before the DR catch-ups.

Recommended Catch-up selections: See this section for more information.


Note: Research Focus and Catch-up are seperate!


Optimal Research Academy Projects

Notes:

  • Projects ARE rated within a tier!

  • For D researches, Rainbow Ship Blueprints > Gold Ship Blueprints, regardless of the research length / rarity color.

  • Only spend cubes on research if you have a large (400+ cubes or higher) surplus, and are willing to risk your gacha chances.

  • Avoid taking H-type or D-type projects once all ships in that season are maxed. The protocore gained is not worth it.

  • Always have a research running at all times, even if it's bad!

Location

  • 1 PR Print -> 5 Protocore

  • 1 DR Print -> 20 Protocore
